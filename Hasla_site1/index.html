Geolocation and Proximity Matching

Our platform uses advanced geolocation technology to match job opportunities with workers in close proximity. This reduces travel time and costs, making it easier for workers to take on more jobs and for clients to get their tasks completed quickly.

Inclusive Access to Employment

By lowering the barriers to entry, Hasla provides job opportunities for a wide range of individuals, including those without formal education or extensive work experience. This inclusivity fosters economic mobility and empowers more people to contribute to their communities.

About Us

Hasla is an innovative mobile application dedicated to revolutionizing the on-demand labor market in Sub-Saharan Africa and globally. Founded on the vision of addressing the high unemployment rates and providing meaningful job opportunities, Hasla connects individuals and businesses with local labor talent for immediate, short-term tasks.

How did we come up with Hasla?

High Unemployment Rates

Sub-Saharan Africa faces an unemployment rate of 7.2%, significantly above the global average. Countries like South Africa, Djibouti, and Eswatini experience even higher rates, reaching up to 32.1%.

Barriers to Entry for Job Seekers

Traditional job markets often have high barriers to entry, including lengthy application processes and requirements for formal education. Hasla lowers these barriers, allowing anyone with the ability to work to find opportunities.

Lack of Immediate Job Platforms .

There is a notable absence of reliable platforms that connect individuals to immediate, short-term labor opportunities, leaving many potential workers and employers disconnected. .
